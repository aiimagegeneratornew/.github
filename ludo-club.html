Strategy Tips

Master the game with these essential Ludo strategies:

  • Focus on getting multiple pieces out of the home base early
  • Create blockades to protect your pieces and hinder opponents
  • Calculate risk vs. reward when deciding which piece to move
  • Pay attention to opponent patterns and adapt your strategy
  • Use safe squares strategically to protect important pieces
