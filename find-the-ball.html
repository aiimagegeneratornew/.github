Tips for Success

Master Find the Ball with these essential strategies:

  • Focus on the initial position of the ball before movement begins
  • Track the ball's movement pattern rather than individual cups
  • Practice at slower speeds before attempting faster levels
  • Take regular breaks to maintain peak concentration
  • Use peripheral vision to catch subtle movements
